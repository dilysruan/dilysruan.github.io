Research

My research focuses on answering questions in cosmology (expansion rate of the Universe, dark matter, galaxy formation) through the tool of gravitational lensing. Currently I am studying how to improve measurements of the Hubble constant through free form lens models of strongly lensed quasars. My advisor is Dr. Chuck Keeton. I am a member of the LSST Strong Lensing Science Collaboration. Previously in undergrad, I studied radio pulsars using the Expanded Long Wavelength Array with the advisement of Dr. Greg Taylor.

Here's a list of my publications on ADS, and here are some of my selected publications:
