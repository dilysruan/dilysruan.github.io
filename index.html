Hello! I'm Dilys Ruan.

I'm interested in exploring cosmology questions through the tool of gravitational lensing and hydrodynamic cosmological simulations.

Research

Here's a list of my publications on ADS.

Research interests: cosmology, strong lensing, dark matter, small-scale structure. I have experience working with pygravlens, lensmodel, emcee, and simulations like IllustrisTNG.

Currently I'm working with Chuck Keeton, focusing on free-form lens models to improve measurements of the Hubble constant through strongly lensed quasars by massive elliptical galaxies. I'm also interested in learning the nature of dark matter through small scales, which can be done through strong lensing and weak lensing signals from dwarf galaxies. In undergrad, I studied radio pulsars using the Expanded Long Wavelength Array with Dr. Greg Taylor. I also compared deconvolution and masking algorithms in radio synthesis imaging with Kumar Golap and Takahiro Tsutsumi during the NRAO REU program.
