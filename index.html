About Me

I am a second-generation Chinese American. I grew up in Albuquerque, NM and am very fond of my hometown! My other interests include music, drawing, and tea/coffee.
I earned my B.S. degree in Astrophysics at the University of New Mexico, and currently I am a PhD Candidate in the Dept of Physics of Astronomy at Rutgers University.
My first name is pronounced like "D"+"Elise" and my last name is pronounced like "Roo"+"awn". I use she/her pronouns.

I believe it is vital to make physics and higher education more accessible to anyone interested. I am a member of the Rutgers Minorities in Physics and Astronomy (MiPA) group and serve as the co-organizer of our Equity and Inclusion Journal Club.
